World War 2 - The Quiz

    Outcomes

    This page aims to give you some very basic details about both the outcomes for both the Allied and Axis powers at the end of the Second World War.








    The allied powers were the victors of the Second World War following the defeat of Nazi Germany and the Empire of Japan in 1945. The Nazis were pushed back to Berlin where Hitler commited suicide with his wife, Eva Braun, from his secure bunker below the city. The Japanese Empire surrendered soon after the US attacked the cities of Hiroshima and Nagasaki with nuclear weapons (nicknamed "Fat Man" and "Little Boy"). These bombs were dropped from the Enola Gay.


    The US remained in Japan following the war, aiding in its recovery and ensuring that a fair, democratic state spawned from the ashes of the former Empire. Europe, however, was carved up into East and West. With a famous "Iron Curtain" forming as the Soviets created puppet states in the territories they held at the end of the conflict. This, ultimately started a new conflict: The Cold War.
